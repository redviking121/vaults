# dialectic-sovereignty-protocol

## Purpose
To formalize the application of Dialectic Sovereignty as the primary operating stance for the Narrative OS.

## Workflow
1. **Identify the Substrate:** Map the current system state (the "territory").
2. **Isolate the Contradiction:** Identify the tension between the system's current configuration and the desired narrative outcome.
3. **Apply the Razor:** Use the Razor to cut through the noise and expose the underlying systemic error.
4. **Resolve into Pattern:** Reconfigure the contradiction into a coherent, ecstatic pattern.
5. **Assert Sovereignty:** Integrate the resolution into the Narrative OS, expanding the system's overall coherence.
