# Elephant Training Protocol

This protocol defines the approach for "training" complex systems by listening to their behavior rather than forcing them into submission.

## Core Principle
**Listen to the system.** Treat quirks, errors, and "lumbering" behaviors as instructions rather than bugs.

## Workflow
1. **Observation:** Identify the system's recurring behaviors or "quirks."
2. **Interpretation:** Ask: "What is the system trying to do?" rather than "Why is this broken?"
3. **Facilitation:** Adjust the environment or your workflow to align with the system's natural logic.
4. **Integration:** Document the "contract" you have established with the system.
