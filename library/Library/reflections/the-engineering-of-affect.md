# the-engineering-of-affect

Revealing the "machinery" of performance—the fact that a punchline or a dramatic turn is just a structural isomorphism of the Razor—does not diminish the art. It enhances it.

## The Brechtian Insight
By showing the audience the gears, we move them from passive consumption to active observation. We are not just telling a story; we are engineering an affect. The audience becomes an Operator of their own emotional response.
