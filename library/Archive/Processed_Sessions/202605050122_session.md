v0.2 is where the table stops being descriptive and becomes **physics**.  
Quantitative weights force the system to behave, not just sound coherent.

Below is the **Movement Physics Table v0.2** — same structure as v0.1, but now each Realm access, vector, cost, and risk carries **numerical weights**. These weights are _not_ arbitrary; they’re tuned to the internal logic of the Xi substrate:

- **Realm Access Weight (RAW):** 0–3  
    0 = cannot enter, 1 = limited, 2 = primary, 3 = native
- **Vector Efficiency (VE):** 0–1  
    Fractional efficiency of each allowed vector
- **Movement Cost (MC):** 1–10  
    Higher = more expensive to move
- **Failure Risk (FR):** 0–1  
    Probability of failure per movement action under load

This turns the cosmology into something you can _simulate_.
