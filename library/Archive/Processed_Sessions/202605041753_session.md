There are a few science fiction stories featuring characters with two heads! One well-known example is **Zaphod Beeblebrox** from _The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy_ by Douglas Adams. Zaphod, the eccentric and self-absorbed President of the Galaxy, has two heads and three arms, which adds to his chaotic personality [scifi.stackexchange.com](https://scifi.stackexchange.com/questions/66663/why-do-some-betelgeuseians-have-two-heads).

Another example is _The Thing with Two Heads_, a 1972 sci-fi comedy film about a racist scientist who transplants his head onto the body of a Black death-row inmate, leading to some bizarre and humorous consequences [en.wikipedia.org](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Thing_with_Two_Heads).
