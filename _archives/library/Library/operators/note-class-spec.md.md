# Note Class Specification

## Purpose
Define the taxonomy of note classes used in the Library.

---

## Session
**Purpose:** Capture real‑time work, decisions, and next actions.  
**Filename:** YYYYMMDD-HHmm_session.md  
**Fields:** Summary, Notes, Decisions, Next Actions  
**When to Use:** Any synchronous or asynchronous working session.

---

## RFC
**Purpose:** Propose, refine, or ratify structural changes.  
**Filename:** RFC-YYYYMMDD-XXXX.md  
**Fields:** Problem, Proposal, Rationale, Impact, Status  
**When to Use:** Any change to architecture, doctrine, or workflow.

---

## Brief
**Purpose:** Short, high‑signal explanation of a concept or decision.  
**Filename:** BRIEF-YYYYMMDD-XXXX.md  
**Fields:** Context, Explanation, Implications  
**When to Use:** When clarity is needed quickly.

---

## Log
**Purpose:** Chronological record of events, actions, or observations.  
**Filename:** LOG-YYYYMMDD-HHmm.md  
**Fields:** Entry  
**When to Use:** Tracking, monitoring, or recording sequences.

---

## Scratch
**Purpose:** Temporary thinking space.  
**Filename:** SCRATCH-YYYYMMDD-HHmm.md  
**Fields:** Freeform  
**When to Use:** Exploration, drafting, ideation.

---

## Index
**Purpose:** Curated entry point into a domain.  
**Filename:** INDEX-<topic>.md  
**Fields:** Overview, Links, Structure  
**When to Use:** When a topic becomes large enough to need a map.
