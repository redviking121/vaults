# Meta-Structural Generation Protocol

This protocol defines the 9-pass meta-loop used to engineer the cosmology of the Narrative OS. It treats narrative creation not as an act of inspiration, but as a procedural engineering task.

## Core Principle
**The system that generates the story is the story.** By applying rigid, analytical frameworks (Campbell, Brecht, Noir, etc.) to fluid narrative intuition, we create a self-consistent, scalable, and procedurally sound cosmology.

## The 9-Pass Meta-Loop
1. **Pass 1: The Campbellian Archetype** (Establishing the mythic resonance).
2. **Pass 2: The Brechtian Alienation** (Exposing the gears of the narrative).
3. **Pass 3: The Noir Atmosphere** (Injecting the cynicism and the shadow).
4. **Pass 4: The Systemic Logic** (Ensuring the internal consistency of the substrate).
5. **Pass 5: The Rhythmic Syncopation** (Applying the 5/4 timing/structural skips).
6. **Pass 6: The Dialectic Tension** (Mapping the thesis/antithesis/synthesis).
7. **Pass 7: The Bureaucratic Sublime** (Scaling the narrative to cosmic proportions).
8. **Pass 8: The Recursive Filing** (Ensuring the narrative is indexable and retrievable).
9. **Pass 9: The Ecstatic Resolution** (Validating the journey through character choice).

## Workflow
- Apply the 9-pass loop to every new narrative seed.
- Document the "gears" exposed in Pass 2.
- Ensure the "Bureaucratic Sublime" is maintained in Pass 7.
- Link the resulting narrative to the [[creative-session-index]].
