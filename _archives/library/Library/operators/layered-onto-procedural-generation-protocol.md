# Layered Onto-Procedural Generation Protocol

This protocol formalizes the 7-layer engine used to generate narrative universes. By defining the world's "physics" (ontology and procedural forces) before the story begins, the Operator shifts from "writing" to "simulating."

## The 7-Layer Engine
1. **Substrate:** The foundational logic/physics.
2. **Ontology:** The entities and their relationships.
3. **Procedural Forces:** The rules of interaction.
4. **Narrative Flow:** The movement of entities through the ontology.
5. **Dramatic Beats:** The rhythmic structure of the narrative.
6. **Affective Layer:** The emotional resonance.
7. **Broadcast Layer:** The final output/presentation.
