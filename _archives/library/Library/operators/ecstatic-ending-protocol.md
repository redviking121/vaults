# Ecstatic Ending Protocol

This protocol formalizes "triumph through choice" as the ultimate narrative resolution.

## Core Principle
Replace the expected "tragic swell" or "solemn silence" with a ripple of laughter and a harmonic of joy. The characters have not been destroyed; they have achieved a state of absolute, chosen freedom.

## Workflow
1. **Identify Agency:** Ensure the resolution is a direct result of character choice.
2. **Invert Emotion:** Shift the audience's expectation from grief/tragedy to triumph/joy.
3. **Validate the Journey:** Ensure the ending validates the entire narrative arc as a process of liberation.
