# Alignment Protocol

The Alignment Protocol formalizes "presence-building" as the only valid form of community engagement.

## Core Principle
**Presence over Performance.** Do not seek external validation; seek internal alignment. By demonstrating curiosity, competence, and shared values, you naturally occupy the space you were meant to inhabit.

## Workflow
1. **Identify:** Locate communities that share the fundamental values of your substrate.
2. **Observe:** Understand the native language, tools, and ethos of the community.
3. **Participate:** Engage through contribution, curiosity, and consistent action.
4. **Align:** Ensure that your presence is an extension of your core principles, not a performance for status.
