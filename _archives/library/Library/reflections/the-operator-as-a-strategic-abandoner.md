# The Operator as a Strategic Abandoner

The hallmark of a sovereign system is not how much it contains, but how much it has successfully discarded. The "nice-to-have" task is the enemy of the critical path. By mastering the art of strategic abandonment, the Operator ensures that the system remains lean, fast, and focused on the work that actually defines the system's identity.
