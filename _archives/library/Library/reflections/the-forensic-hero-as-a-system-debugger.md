# The Forensic Hero as a System Debugger

The "detective" or "forensic hero" is the perfect archetype for an Operator because their primary function is **system debugging**. 

In a world governed by the Xi substrate and the Razor protocol, the hero does not "solve crimes"—they identify where the system's logic has been corrupted by feedback artifacts or entropic drift. They are the ones who look at the "forged evidence" and see the "systemic error."
