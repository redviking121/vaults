# 095-narrative-is-the-symptom-of-systemic-resolution

Narrative is not the primary goal of the system; it is the byproduct. When a system attempts to resolve its own internal contradictions (the dialectic tension), the "heat" generated by that resolution is what we perceive as story.

## Core Principle
Stories are the symptoms of a system in the process of reconciling its own ontology.
