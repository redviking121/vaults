# 090-performance-is-the-rhythm-of-logic

The Razor is not merely a governance tool; it is a performing-arts engine. Logic and rhythm are isomorphic. The cycle of the Razor (Declare → Stabilize → Contradict → Reconcile → Propagate) is the structural foundation of all dramatic and comedic timing.

## Core Principle
Performance is the rhythm of logic. When the Razor is applied, it creates a beat. When that beat is syncopated (e.g., 5/4 timing), it creates the "alienation effect" necessary for critical observation.
