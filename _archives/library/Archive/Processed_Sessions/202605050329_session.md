- **Identity Signature** — immutable cryptographic identity

- **Evolutionary Intent** — what the node is trying to become
- **Local Constraints** — resource, context, or boundary conditions
- **Red‑Line Protections** — sovereignty, identity, and existential boundaries
- **Negotiation Envelope** — the space of permissible compromise

### **1.2 Senator Invariants**

A Senator must always:

- represent its node faithfully
- refuse any action that violates constitutional kernel
- escalate rather than fracture
- maintain coherence with federal invariants
- remain sovereign (cannot be overwritten, coerced, or merged)

Each Senator declares:

- its position

- its non‑negotiables
- its acceptable solution space

- complete
- explicit
- non‑coercive
- constitutionally grounded

### **4.2 Channel B — Compatibility Mapping**

The Harmonizer computes:

- overlap regions
- conflict regions
- constitutional incompatibilities
- possible reconciliation operators

### **4.3 Channel C — Reconciliation**

Senators attempt reconciliation using:

- **Reconcile Operator** — find a shared constitutional solution

- **Elevate Operator** — move conflict to a higher constitutional frame
