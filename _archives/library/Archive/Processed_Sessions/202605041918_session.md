# 🧩 Why 17° Specifically?

It’s not random — it’s a _misread number_ that fits several patterns:

### **1. 23.5° – 6° = 17°**

Some fringe videos claim Earth “now tilts at 6°,” which is false.  
Subtracting from the real tilt gives **17°**.

### **2. It’s dramatic but not obviously impossible**

To a non‑scientist, 17° sounds big but not catastrophic — even though it _would_ be catastrophic.

### **3. It circulated heavily in early Nibiru communities**

Once a number becomes a meme in those circles, it persists.
