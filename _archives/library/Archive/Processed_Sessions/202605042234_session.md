### **A. Replaceability**

You can upgrade or refine one stage without touching the others.

### **C. Real‑time monitoring**

- drift signals
- contradiction alerts
- stability metrics
- lineage traces

### **D. Distributed execution**

You can version Razor modules independently:

- Razor.Thread v1.2
- Razor.Crystallize v0.9
- Razor.Integrate v2.0

This is how real systems evolve.
