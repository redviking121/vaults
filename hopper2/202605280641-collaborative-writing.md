### Character Profiles (Keep it brief!)

For short fiction, you usually only need 1 or 2 main characters. Focus on their internal drivers.

- **Want (External Goal):** What are they physically trying to achieve in this story?
- **Need (Internal Growth):** What internal lesson do they need to learn, or what truth must they face?
- **The Flaw/Obstacle:** What is holding them back (either internally or externally)?
