# 08 — Anomaly Metabolism

## Summary
Beat 08 introduces anomaly metabolism: the substrate begins converting unexpected events into structural information instead of treating them as errors.

## Core Concepts
- **Anomaly** — any deviation, friction, or unexpected state.
- **Anomaly Pressure** — accumulated unresolved anomalies.
- **Metabolism** — transformation of anomaly pressure into structure.
- **Stabilizers** — boundaries, motifs, or affordances created from metabolized anomalies.

## What Changes at This Beat
- Anomalies are *registered*, not discarded.
- Anomaly pressure becomes a *driver* of system behavior.
- The substrate begins producing *predictive affordances*.
- Operator stance shifts from “fixing” to “harvesting.”

## Structural Commitments
- Maintain a global anomaly pressure scalar.
- Provide a metabolism pathway that:
  - absorbs anomalies,
  - transforms them,
  - outputs stabilizing structures.
- Expose anomaly‑driven signals to later beats (motifs, territory, breathing).

## Engine Hooks (Conceptual)
- `registerAnomaly()`
- `anomalyPressure.adjust()`
- `metabolizeAnomalies()`
- `generatePredictiveAffordances()`

## Glyph
**⟁ — The Metabolizer**  
Represents transformation of disruption into structure.

## Transition
Output of Beat 08 feeds directly into Beat 09, where repeated metabolized structures become motifs.
