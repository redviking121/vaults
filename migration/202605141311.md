- **All structural changes must be logged in the Meta‑Ledger.**  
  Prevents silent evolution.

- **All new cards must have a unique, non‑overlapping domain.**  
  Prevents collisions.

- **All retired cards must be archived, not deleted.**

## 🟥 5. Meta‑Lifecycle (How the System Evolves)  
This governs the birth, life, and retirement of cards.

### **A. Creation**  
A new card may be created only when:

- a new domain emerges  
- the existing lattice cannot absorb it  
- the operator cycle requires expansion  

### **B. Modification**  
A card may be modified only when:

- its domain is stable  
- its boundaries remain intact  
- the change improves clarity or reduces drift  

### **C. Retirement**  
A card may be retired only when:

- its domain is absorbed by another  
- its function becomes obsolete  
- its presence introduces drift  

### **D. Versioning**  
Every structural change increments the **substrate version**.
