# **Ξ Illustrated Map of the Four Realms (Razor Cosmology v0.1)**

## **🜂 The East — Realm of Origins (Zotero, Capture, Logs)**

**Terrain:**

- jagged mountains of raw data
- river deltas of highlights
- fog banks of half‑formed thoughts
- scattered shrines of “first contact” moments

**Landmarks:**

- **The Annotation Fields** — where PDFs shed their fragments
- **The River of Highlights** — flowing westward, always downhill
- **The Shrine of First Insight** — where the Pilgrim awakens
- **The Gate of Ingestion** — guarded by the Monk of Normalization

**Inhabitants:**

- The **Pilgrim** (the note)

**Function:**
