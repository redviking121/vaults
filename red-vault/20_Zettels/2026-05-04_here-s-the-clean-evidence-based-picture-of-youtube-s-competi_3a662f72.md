## Major Competitors in the Online Video Space

### 1. **Dailymotion**

A long‑standing YouTube alternative with a mix of professional and amateur content. It attracts over 300 million monthly users and offers monetization, though with stricter upload limits. [Tech 21 Century](https://www.tech21century.com/best-video-sharing-sites-like-youtube/)

### 2. **Vimeo**

A creator‑centric platform emphasizing high‑quality, artistic, and professional video. Strong in filmmaking, design, and business use cases, with subscription‑based monetization and advanced privacy tools. [Business Model Analyst](https://businessmodelanalyst.com/youtube-competitors/)

### 3. **Twitch**

Primarily a livestreaming platform, especially for gaming, but increasingly a competitor for long‑form creator content. [Business Model Analyst](https://businessmodelanalyst.com/youtube-competitors/)

### 4. **Instagram (including IGTV)**

A social‑video competitor with strong mobile‑first engagement. Native video and IGTV make it a discovery‑driven alternative. [Business Model Analyst](https://businessmodelanalyst.com/youtube-competitors/)

### 5. **TikTok**

Dominant in short‑form video, algorithmic discovery, and creator virality. A major competitor for attention even though its format differs from YouTube’s long‑form roots. [Business Model Analyst](https://businessmodelanalyst.com/youtube-competitors/)

### 6. **Rumble**

A fast‑growing alternative with a focus on creator monetization and less algorithmic filtering. [Business Model Analyst](https://businessmodelanalyst.com/youtube-competitors/)

### 7. **Odysee (LBRY)**

A decentralized, blockchain‑based video platform appealing to creators who want more control and fewer platform restrictions. [Business Model Analyst](https://businessmodelanalyst.com/youtube-competitors/)

### 8. **Tubi**

A free, ad‑supported streaming service—less of a creator platform, but still a competitor for viewer attention. [Business Model Analyst](https://businessmodelanalyst.com/youtube-competitors/)

### 9. **DTube**

A decentralized, ad‑free platform built on blockchain, positioning itself as a censorship‑resistant alternative. [Business Model Analyst](https://businessmodelanalyst.com/youtube-competitors/)

### 10. **Utreon**

A newer platform designed explicitly as a YouTube alternative, with subscription‑based creator support and fewer algorithmic constraints. [Tech 21 Century](https://www.tech21century.com/best-video-sharing-sites-like-youtube/)
