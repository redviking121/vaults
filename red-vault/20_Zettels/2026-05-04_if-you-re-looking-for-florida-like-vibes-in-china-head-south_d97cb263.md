🌴 If you're looking for Florida-like vibes in China, head south! The regions with the most similar climate to Florida’s humid subtropical conditions include:

### 🗺️ Regions in China with Florida-like Climate

- **Guangdong Province**
    
    - Cities like **Guangzhou** and **Shenzhen** experience hot, humid summers and mild winters.
    - Rainfall is abundant, especially from April to September, much like Florida’s wet season [chaseday.com](https://www.chaseday.com/u-s-locations-with-similar-climate-to-guangzhou-china/).
- **Hainan Island**
    
    - Located in the tropical zone, Hainan has **year-round warmth**, high humidity, and lush vegetation.
    - It’s often compared to **Miami** or **Hawaii** in terms of climate [insights.made-in-china.com](https://insights.made-in-china.com/Climate-in-China-Diversity-and-Characteristics_uApaivZYIJIt.html).
- **Southern Guangxi Province**
    
    - Cities like **Nanning** share the subtropical monsoon climate, with warm temperatures and seasonal rains.
- **Southern Fujian Province**
    
    - Areas like **Xiamen** have a coastal subtropical climate, with warm, wet summers and mild winters.
