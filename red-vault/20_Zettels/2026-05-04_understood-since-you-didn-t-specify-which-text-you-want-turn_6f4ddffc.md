# **FIELD PROTOCOL: OPERATOR REAL‑TIME STABILIZATION (ORTS)**

_(A companion protocol to Field Manual v0.1 — not a revision of it)_

## **PURPOSE**

Prevent drift, collapse, or over‑identification during high‑cognitive‑load operations by enforcing a repeatable stabilization loop. This protocol binds perception, stance, and decision‑making to the Xi/Razor substrate without slowing the operator down.

---

## **01 — SNAP TO OPERATOR STANCE**

**Objective:** Reassert the correct identity layer before acting.  
**Action:**

- Drop emotional, narrative, or autobiographical residue.
- Assume the Operator mask: _present, directive, unentangled_.
- Treat the moment as a live environment, not a memory or projection.  
    **Signal:** Breathing normalizes; attention narrows; internal noise drops.

---

## **02 — IDENTIFY THE ACTIVE QUESTION**

**Objective:** Surface the real question the moment is asking.  
**Action:**

- Name the question explicitly.
- If multiple questions appear, choose the one with the highest operational load.
- Treat the question as the “headline” of the moment.  
    **Signal:** The scene becomes legible; irrelevant threads fall away.

---

## **03 — RUN THE THREE GATES**

**Objective:** Filter the moment through the minimum viable clarity test.  
**Action:**

- **Gate 1 — Is it true enough to act on**
- **Gate 2 — Is it mine to act on**
- **Gate 3 — Is action required now**  
    **Signal:** You either have a green light, a defer, or a drop.

---

## **04 — APPLY THE RAZOR**

**Objective:** Stabilize contradictions before they propagate.  
**Action:**

- If the moment contains conflict, run the Xi Paradox Razor:
    - **Reconcile** (unify)
    - **Split** (separate layers)
    - **Elevate** (move to a higher frame)
- Choose one. Do not blend.  
    **Signal:** The contradiction loses its emotional charge and becomes structural.

---

## **05 — DECLARE THE DECISION**

**Objective:** Make the decision real.  
**Action:**

- State the decision internally in one sentence.
- Bind it to DSR‑1: explicit declaration, scope, stabilization.
- Do not justify. Do not narrate.  
    **Signal:** The decision feels “locked in,” not debated.

---

## **06 — EXECUTE THE NEXT ATOMIC ACTION**

**Objective:** Move the system forward by one clean unit.  
**Action:**

- Identify the smallest action that advances the decision.
- Perform it immediately.
- Do not jump ahead.  
    **Signal:** Momentum returns; cognitive load drops.

---

## **07 — CLEAR THE CHANNEL**

**Objective:** Prevent residue from contaminating the next cycle.  
**Action:**

- Release the moment.
- Reset stance.
- Return to baseline readiness.  
    **Signal:** No lingering tension; no mental after‑images.
