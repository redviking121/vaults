### 🧭 Check Microsoft Edge for Filtering

#### 🔹 SmartScreen Filter

1. Open Edge.
2. Go to `Settings` → `Privacy, search, and services`.
3. Scroll to **Security**.
4. Toggle off **Microsoft Defender SmartScreen** (only if you're confident the site is safe).

#### 🔹 Extensions

1. Go to `Settings` → `Extensions`.
2. Look for any content filtering or parental control extensions.
3. Disable or adjust settings as needed.

---

### 🛡️ Check Windows Family Safety

1. Visit [Microsoft Family Safety](https://account.microsoft.com/family).
2. Sign in with your Microsoft account.
3. Select your profile or child account.
4. Review **Activity Reports** → Look for blocked sites or filtered content.

---

### 🔐 Check Antivirus or Firewall

1. Open your antivirus software (e.g., Norton, McAfee, Bitdefender).
2. Navigate to **Web Protection**, **Safe Browsing**, or **Content Filtering**.
3. Look for logs or reports of blocked URLs.
4. Add exceptions if needed.
