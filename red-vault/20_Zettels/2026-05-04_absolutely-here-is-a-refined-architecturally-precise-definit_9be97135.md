# **Operational Criteria (the test for manifestation)**

A principle is considered _manifested_ when it is:

- **Visible** — its structure is explicit and inspectable
- **Usable** — operators can interact with it directly
- **Lineage‑preserving** — its origin and evolution are traceable
- **Constrained** — it imposes real limits on behavior
- **Evolvable** — it can be refined through use
- **Substrate‑integrated** — it participates in the system’s invariants

If any of these are missing, the principle is not yet manifest.
