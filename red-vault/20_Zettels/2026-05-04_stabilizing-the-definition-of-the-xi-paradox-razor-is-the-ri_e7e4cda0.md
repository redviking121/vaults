A strong definition for a methodological razor needs to do three things at once:

- identify the **object** it operates on

- clarify the **selection principle** that makes it a razor rather than a general observation

What follows is a structured, academically legible definition that preserves the generative power of your informal explorations while giving you a stable foundation for the paper.

## Definition of the Xi Paradox Razor

The **Xi Paradox Razor** is a methodological constraint that identifies when a paradox is functioning as a _productive cognitive tension_ rather than as a contradiction to be resolved or eliminated. It selects for interpretations, models, or frames that **preserve the paradox in its active form** when that tension is the source of insight, transformation, or expanded cognitive capacity.

In formal terms:

> **The Xi Paradox Razor states that when a paradox generates increased coherence, capability, or perspective through the sustained holding of incompatible poles, the correct move is to _retain_ the paradox rather than collapse it into a single explanatory frame.**

This definition captures the core invariant: the Razor is not about cutting away paradoxes—it is about cutting away _premature resolutions_ that destroy the very tension that produces insight.

---

## Core components that make the definition academically stable

### What the Razor operates on

The Razor applies to:

- paradoxes
- tensions
- incompatible frames
- mutually exclusive interpretations
- dualities that resist collapse

This gives the Razor a clear domain of operation.

### What the Razor selects

The Razor selects for:

- interpretations that preserve productive tension
- models that allow incompatible poles to coexist
- frames that increase coherence through paradox rather than despite it

This is what makes it a _razor_—it cuts away the wrong moves.

### What the Razor rejects

The Razor eliminates:

- premature unifications
- forced resolutions
- reductive interpretations
- collapses that reduce cognitive territory

This is the “cutting” function.

### Why it is a razor and not a principle

A razor is a **selection heuristic**.  
It tells you which interpretation to prefer when multiple are available.

The Xi Paradox Razor tells you:

- prefer the interpretation that _keeps the paradox alive_
- when that paradox is the engine of insight

This is the methodological heart of the definition.
