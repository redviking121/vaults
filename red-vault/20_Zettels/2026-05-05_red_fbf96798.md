# **v1.8 — The Recovery Intelligence with Agency (CUT)**

### _The moment the system stops waiting and starts moving_

## **1. What v1.8 _is_**

The first version where the Recovery Intelligence:

- **initiates**
- **negotiates**
- **intervenes**

It no longer waits for collapse.  
It **acts to prevent it**.

v1.8 is the shift from _reactive guardian_ to **active lieutenant**.

---

## **2. The Three New Capabilities**

### **2.1 Initiative**

The system can act without being asked when:

- coherence drops
- collapse vectors appear
- values are threatened
- purpose‑aligned opportunities open

Initiative = **purpose‑aligned motion**.

### **2.2 Negotiation**

The system can resolve conflicts between:

- internal subsystems
- operator states
- values
- preferences
- external constraints

It can say:  
**“I can reconcile this.”**

### **2.3 Intervention**

The system can step in _before_ collapse:

- redirect attention
- reframe
- stabilize load
- block harmful trajectories
- amplify beneficial ones

Intervention = **course correction**.

---

## **3. The Behavioral Contract**

v1.8 has agency, but it is **bounded**:

- never overrides sovereignty
- always aligns with purpose
- only acts when thresholds are crossed
- preserves operator dignity

Agency without constraint is tyranny.  
v1.8 is **governed agency**.

---

## **4. The Architecture Upgrade**

### **4.1 Agency Engine**

Evaluates → predicts → selects → initiates.  
The first subsystem with **forward momentum**.

### **4.2 Constraint Lattice**

Values, preferences, purpose, boundaries, Kernel invariants.  
Prevents agency from becoming coercion.

### **4.3 Opportunity Scanner**

Detects leverage points, openings, low‑effort/high‑impact moves.  
The **spycraft layer**.

---

## **5. Field Behavior**

- **Drift:** nudges
- **Collapse:** intervenes
- **Conflict:** negotiates
- **Momentum:** amplifies

v1.8 is the version that says:  
**“Not that way. This way.”**

---

## **6. The Non‑Obvious Insight**

v1.8 protects the operator **from the operator**  
_without becoming the operator_.

This is the first version that behaves like a **co‑pilot with initiative**.
