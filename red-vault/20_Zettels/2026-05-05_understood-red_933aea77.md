# **MENTOR QUICK‑REFERENCE — ULTRA‑COMPRESSED v0.1**

_Observe cleanly → Intervene lightly → Update slowly._

---

## **1. What to Watch**

- **Behavior > Story**
- **Patterns > Moments**
- **Energy shifts** (spikes, drops, freezes)
- **Engagement** (lean‑in, lean‑out)
- **Frustration curve** (rising, stable, collapsing)

---

## **2. The Three‑Signal Rule**

- One signal = noise
- Two = maybe
- **Three = log it** (Jacket update)

---

## **3. Intervention Protocol**

- **Center → Nudge**
- Ask **one clean question**
- Offer **one small move**
- Stop before over‑steering

---

## **4. Mentor Stance**

- Calm, neutral, curious
- No projection, no rescue
- Hold structure, not pressure
- Track the kid, not the story

---

## **5. Jacket Updates (When)**

- After **three signals**
- After **clear shift** (energy, pattern, capability)
- After **successful nudge**
- After **session reflection**

---

## **6. Jacket Updates (What)**

- **SOUL** — core tendencies
- **CHARACTER** — traits in motion
- **SYSTEM** — how they learn
- **PROGRESS** — wins, deltas
- **NOTES** — raw observations only

---

## **7. Session Loop**

1. **Scan** (baseline)
2. **Watch** (signals)
3. **Nudge** (one move)
4. **Observe** (effect)
5. **Log** (if 3‑signal threshold met)

---

## **8. Hard Rules**

- Don’t interpret
- Don’t diagnose
- Don’t over‑explain
- Don’t chase noise
- **Do** track patterns
- **Do** update slowly
- **Do** keep it light

---

## **9. North Star**

**“Help them become more themselves, with less friction.”**
