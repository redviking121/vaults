- Dee: ontological layers
- Xi: cognitive layers  
    Both treat _layering_ as the fundamental architecture of meaning.

### 🜂 Angelic Mediation → Xi’s Interface Function

In Dee’s system, angels are not supernatural beings so much as **interfaces**—intelligences that mediate between human cognition and higher-order structure.  
In Xi, the interface is internal: the practitioner becomes the mediator, holding multiple frames and allowing insight to emerge from their tension.

- Dee externalizes the interface
- Xi internalizes it  
    But the function is the same: **bridging incompatible domains**.

### 🜃 Mathematical Harmony → Xi’s Structural Integrity

Dee believed mathematics was the grammar of creation. Xi uses structural integrity—coherence, tension, continuity—as its grammar.  
Both systems assume that **order is discoverable through structure**, not dogma.
