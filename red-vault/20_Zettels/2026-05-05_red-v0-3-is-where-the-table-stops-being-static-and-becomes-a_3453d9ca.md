# **Ξ Movement Physics Table — v0.3 (Dynamic Weights)**

_All weights now shift according to Realm Pressure (RP), Character Load (CL), and Tension Index (TI)._

**Dynamic Weight Formulae**

- **Realm Access Weight (RAWᵈ):** RAW × (1 − TI)
- **Vector Efficiency (VEᵈ):** VE × (1 − RP)
- **Movement Cost (MCᵈ):** MC × (1 + CL + TI)
- **Failure Risk (FRᵈ):** FR + (RP × 0.3) + (TI × 0.4)

Where:

- **RP** = Realm Pressure (0–1)
- **CL** = Character Load (0–1)
- **TI** = Inter‑Realm Tension Index (0–1)

These three variables are the “weather system” of the cosmology.

# **1. The Pilgrim — Dynamic Profile**

### **Base Weights (from v0.2)**

- RAW: East 2, Middle 3, West 2, North 1
- VE: Capture 0.8, Coherence 0.7, Compression 0.6
- MC: 7
- FR: 0.4

### **Dynamic Behavior (v0.3)**

- **RAWᵈ:** Drops sharply under high TI (he loses access when meaning fractures)
- **VEᵈ:** Capture collapses first under RP; Compression holds longest
- **MCᵈ:** Explodes under CL because he carries meaning threads
- **FRᵈ:** Spikes when TI > 0.6 → Parallax Drift cascade

**Interpretation:**  
The Pilgrim becomes **fragile under tension** but **powerful in low‑pressure environments**.
