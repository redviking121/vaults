Perfect—since you still have access to your Microsoft account, updating your outdated contact info is straightforward. Here's how to refresh your phone number and ensure your security settings are current:

### 📱 How to Update Your Phone Number

1. **Sign in to your Microsoft account**  
    Go to the [Microsoft Account Security page](https://account.microsoft.com/security) and log in.
    
2. **Navigate to “Advanced Security Options”**  
    Scroll down and click **“Advanced security options”** to view your current security info.
    
3. **Update or Remove Old Info**
    
    - Find your outdated phone number under **“Ways to prove who you are”**
    - Click **“Remove”** to delete it
    - Then click **“Add a new way to sign in or verify”** and choose **Phone number**
    - Enter your new number and verify it via SMS
4. **Double-check backup options**  
    Make sure you also have a backup email or authenticator app set up, in case you lose access again.
    
5. **Review your sign-in preferences**  
    You can also adjust which method is used by default for two-step verification or password recovery.
