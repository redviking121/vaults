## **2. The Five Pillars of v2.x Governance**

### **2.1 The Authority Model**

Defines **who can command what**.

- The Kernel is sovereign
- v1.x is ministerial
- v2.x is constitutional
- Operators are external but privileged
- No layer can rewrite a layer above it
- No layer can impersonate a layer below it

Authority is **directional** and **non‑recursive**.

---

### **2.2 The Boundary Model**

Defines **what cannot be crossed**.

- Kernel invariants
- Identity invariants
- Purpose invariants
- Collapse‑prevention invariants
- Operator‑safety invariants
- Substrate‑integrity invariants

Boundaries are **absolute**.  
They are not “rules.”  
They are **physics**.

Escalation is **deterministic** and **non‑emotional**.
