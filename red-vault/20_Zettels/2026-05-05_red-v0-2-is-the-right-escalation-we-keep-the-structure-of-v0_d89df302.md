# **Ξ Inter‑Character Recovery Interaction Map — v0.2**

_Rows = Actor in Recovery. Columns = Target’s Recovery State._  
**Values = Base Influence × Stress Function × Integration Coefficient**

Where:

- **RP — Realm Pressure**
- **CL — Character Load**
- **TI — Tension Index**
- **SF(x) = 1 + α·RP + β·CL + γ·TI**
- **IC = integration coefficient** (how much the target _accepts_ or _rejects_ the influence)

# **System‑Level Meaning of v0.2**

v0.2 introduces:

### **1. Stress‑Responsive Recovery**

Recovery is no longer linear.  
It accelerates or collapses depending on RP, CL, and TI.

### **2. Integration Coefficients**

Not all characters _accept_ recovery influence equally.  
This is the first step toward **hysteresis** in v0.4.

### **3. Directional Recovery Fields**

Recovery is now a **vector**, not a scalar.  
Each character emits a different recovery field.
