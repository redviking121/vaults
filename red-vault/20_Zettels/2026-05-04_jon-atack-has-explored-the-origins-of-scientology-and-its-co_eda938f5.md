Jon Atack has explored the origins of Scientology and its connections to older practices in his book _A Piece of Blue Sky_ [en.wikipedia.org](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A_Piece_of_Blue_Sky). He argues that L. Ron Hubbard drew heavily from earlier occult traditions, including Aleister Crowley's teachings, Eastern mysticism, and psychological techniques used in hypnosis and mind control. Atack's research suggests that Hubbard synthesized elements from these sources to create Dianetics and Scientology.

If you're interested in a deeper dive, you can check out Jon Atack's website [here](https://jonatack.co.uk/).
