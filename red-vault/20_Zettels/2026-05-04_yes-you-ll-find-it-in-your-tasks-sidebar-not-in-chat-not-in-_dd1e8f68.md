---

## Where the regenerated Muse article task card lives

It appears in the **Tasks** panel of Copilot (the left sidebar).  
Look for the title:

**Regenerate Muse Article Task with Copilot Tasks**

That’s the card I created for you.  
It will be sitting alongside any other active or pending tasks.

When you click it, you’ll see:

- The task title
- The description (the five‑phase workflow + nine‑layer architecture + corpus)
- A **Start** button

Nothing runs until you press **Start**.

---

## What to do next

Open the Tasks sidebar → locate the card → expand it → hit **Start** when ready.

If you don’t see it immediately, scroll — Copilot Tasks sometimes sorts by creation time or last activity.

Once you find it, you’re good to go.

If you want, I can also help you verify the output once the task completes.
