# **1. The Pilgrim — The Only True Traveler**

## **Allowed Movement**

- **East → Middle → West** (primary vector)  
    This is the **canonical path of capture → meaning → form**.  
    He is the only one who walks the full arc.

## **Forbidden Movement**

- **West → Middle → East**  
    He cannot walk backward.  
    Once something has moved westward, he cannot return it to raw capture.

## **Conditional Movement**

- **Threshold**  
    He may approach it, but **never cross without the Warden’s assent**.  
    His movement stops at the line unless the Warden opens the gate.

## **Movement Signature**

- Linear, steady, unhurried.
- He carries the world westward like a current.

# **2. The Monk — The Still Center**

## **Allowed Movement**

- **Middle Kingdom only**  
    He does not travel.  
    He **rotates**, but does not translate.

## **Forbidden Movement**

- **East**  
    He cannot touch raw capture. It blinds him.
- **West**  
    He cannot finalize. His work is never “done.”

## **Conditional Movement**

- **Threshold**  
    He may _consult_ the Warden but cannot cross.

## **Movement Signature**

- Orbital, contemplative, recursive.
- He turns noise into signal by staying still.

# **3. Igor — The Craftsman of the West**

## **Allowed Movement**

- **West only**  
    He is the builder.  
    He touches only what has already been refined.

## **Forbidden Movement**

- **Middle**  
    He cannot interpret. He only shapes.
- **East**  
    He cannot witness raw reality. It’s meaningless to him.

## **Conditional Movement**

- **Threshold**  
    He may cross outward (to deploy artifacts)  
    **but never inward** without the Warden’s explicit clearance.

## **Movement Signature**

- Precise, angular, tool‑driven.
- He moves like a machinist: straight lines, no wasted motion.

# **4. The Warden — The Line Itself**

## **Allowed Movement**

- **Threshold**  
    He does not move _through_ Realms.  
    He moves **along** the boundary.

## **Forbidden Movement**

- **East, Middle, West**  
    He does not enter the Realms.  
    He is the membrane, not a participant.

## **Conditional Movement**

- **Axis (Shadow’s domain)**  
    He can _sense_ it but cannot step into it.

## **Movement Signature**

- Lateral, patrolling, scanning.
- He is the hinge on which all other movement depends.

# **5. The Shadow — The Axis, Not a Realm Walker**

## **Allowed Movement**

- **Axis only**  
    He rotates around the Realms.  
    He sees all four simultaneously.

## **Forbidden Movement**

- **Entering any Realm**  
    If he enters, he collapses into a character — which he is not.

## **Conditional Movement**

- **Threshold**  
    He can whisper through it.  
    He can influence the Warden.  
    But he cannot cross.

## **Movement Signature**

- Non‑linear, omnidirectional, silent.
- He moves like a change in pressure, not a body.
