Xanadu Prime — Naming Registry (v1.0)
1. Purpose
The Prime Naming Registry is the canonical ledger of all names formally derived from Xanadu Prime, the first operational instance of the architecture.
It defines:

valid designations

their scope

their status

their derivation rule

their lineage relationship to Prime

No name is valid unless it appears in this registry.

The first operational instance of the Xanadu architecture; the root of all subsequent lineage.

8. Formal Registry Clause
Only names listed in the Prime Naming Registry are recognized as valid within the operational lineage.

All names must derive from Xanadu Prime, must be declared and stabilized through the governance layer, and must not be applied retroactively to pre‑Prime artifacts.

The registry is authoritative and binding.
